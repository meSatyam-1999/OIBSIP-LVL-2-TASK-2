A Tribute To


M S DHONI


Biography:


M.S. Dhoni, born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, Bihar (now Jharkhand), India, is widely regarded as one of the greatest cricketers and captains in the history of Indian cricket. He is known for his calm and composed demeanor on the field, his exceptional leadership skills, and his ability to finish matches under pressure. Dhoni's cricketing journey began at a young age when he started playing for local clubs in Ranchi. He made his debut for the Bihar U-19 team in the 1998/99 season and later went on to represent the East Zone U-19 team in the Cooch Behar Trophy. In 2004, he made his debut for the Indian cricket team in an ODI against Bangladesh in Chittagong. Dhoni's rise to stardom was swift, and he soon became a household name in India. He quickly established himself as one of the best finishers in the game, and his ability to remain calm under pressure made him a valuable asset to the team. In 2007, he was appointed as the captain of the Indian T20I team, and he led the team to victory in the inaugural ICC World Twenty20 in South Africa.

Cricket Records:

M.S. Dhoni is widely regarded as one of the greatest cricket captains of all time. He led the Indian cricket team to win several major titles, including the ICC Cricket World Cup 2011, the ICC Champions Trophy 2013, and the Asia Cup 2010 and 2016.
